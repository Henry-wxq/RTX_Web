Professional Operation Team

Established Excellence

Since 2010, Win Trust Information has established its Operation Service Center, building upon our existing implementation services to provide comprehensive operational support to our clients.

Expert Team

Our Operation Service Center boasts one of the industry's top maintenance teams, with extensive implementation and product experience. All our operation consultants undergo rigorous professional training.

Customer-Centric Approach

We strictly adhere to a "customer-first" principle, aiming for the highest user experience and dedicating ourselves completely to serving our clients' needs.

Comprehensive Operation Modes

Flexible Service Models

We offer various operation modes including remote support, on-site service, and hybrid models combining remote and on-site support to meet diverse client needs.

Multi-Application Support

Our professional operation services cover multiple applications (Oracle, Infor) and database maintenance, providing either comprehensive or partial operational support.

Customized Solutions

We provide flexible, customizable service models guided by user requirements and supported by our professional service recommendations to determine the most suitable approach for each client.

Comprehensive Management System

Professional Platform

Our Operation Service Center provides multiple platforms designed to enhance and streamline service delivery for our clients.

Knowledge Management

We maintain a comprehensive knowledge base management system that enables quick access to solutions and best practices.

Problem Management

Our integrated problem management system ensures systematic tracking and resolution of issues, delivering fast and professional support.

Training / Professional System Support

Quality Training Programs

To ensure service quality, we provide various training courses including user training, management training, and system administration training. Through theoretical guidance and practical training, trainees will master system application, maintenance, and management.

Technical Support

We provide comprehensive technical support services including phone support, email support, and on-site support. We believe that technology transfer and experience sharing can help customers utilize system functions more effectively in their environment.

WMS Implementation Training

Win Trust provides Infor WMS implementation consultant training. The training period is one month, divided into four phases:

Phase 1: System Operation Training
Phase 2: Logistics and Warehouse Business Process Training
Phase 3: System Configuration Training
Phase 4: Project Practice Training
