Peace Bird

Advanced Logistics Center for Fashion Retail Distribution

Fashion Retail Cixi, Zhejiang Completed 2023

Project Overview

Total Area 168,000 m²
Warehouse Area 128,000 m²
Project Duration 1.5 years

Logistics Center Functional Areas

Receiving Operations

The center features a dedicated receiving and registration zone where all incoming goods are processed and documented. Adjacent to this is an advanced goods scanning area equipped with state-of-the-art scanning technology to ensure accurate inventory tracking from the moment items enter the facility.

Quality Control

A comprehensive quality inspection zone implements rigorous quality control procedures, where trained personnel examine merchandise against Peace Bird's exacting standards. This area works in tandem with the processing and cleaning area, where garments are prepared for storage or immediate distribution.

Returns Management

The facility includes a specialized returns handling complex, comprising both an inspection and scanning zone for returned items and a dedicated processing area. This systematic approach ensures efficient processing of returns while maintaining inventory accuracy and product quality standards.

Integration and Workflow

All functional areas are seamlessly connected through an automated conveyor system and integrated with the BELT PICKING MODEL, enabling smooth transitions between different processing stages. This integration maximizes operational efficiency and reduces handling time, supporting Peace Bird's fast-fashion business model.
